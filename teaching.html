Overview

Welcome to my teaching portfolio, which showcases my pedagogical development as a graduate instructor at Columbia University. Much of the work was conducted with the Center for Teaching and Learning (CTL).

Teaching Roles & Responsibilities

Computer Engineering Project Instructor

ENGI E1102 The Art of Engineering, Fall 2019 - Spring 2023

  • Conceived project and designed syllabus and assignments based on disciplinary learning objectives
  • Created lesson plans and instructed 4 synchronous workshop-style lessons per semester
  • Developed online content for asynchronous coverage of background material
  • Refined project structure and content based on peer observation and student feedback

Within this required introductory course for first-year engineering undergraduates, students must choose a project (normally led by faculty) in the sub-discipline of their choice. I had the opportunity to develop and instruct a new project. 10-60 students.

Teaching Consultant

Teaching Observation Fellow
